Project Motivation

  • üé¥ Collectible card games (CCG) or Trading card games (TCG) are types of card games that mix strategic deck-building elements with features of trading cards.
  • üÉè One of the earliest and perhaps the biggest in market and volume size is a CCG named Magic: The Gathering.
  • With such an enormous market, the market has problems of its own:
    • Sophisticated counterfeit cards unnoticeable to naked eye
    • Artificial alterations on defects
      • Ink tweaks
      • Rebacked Cards (Splicing 2 Cards together)
    • üõ†Ô∏è Poor Card Conditions (Artifacts)
      • Bends
      • Dents
      • Scratches

Naturally, due to these issues, it is necessary to have a card graded, and authenticated by a human expert before buying or selling. With less availability of human experts and a huge number of customers; as one can imagine, this current pipeline is an extremely time-consuming and expensive process.

In comes, CardVerse: A fully automated machine that authenticates, grades and sorts Magic: The Gathering
